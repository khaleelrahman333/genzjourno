Record-Breaking 106 Journalists Imprisoned In 2024

Credit: Khaleel Rahman

This chart provides a comparison of the detention rates of journalists across different regions during the most recent year, 2024. In many areas, detention incidents remained relatively low. In regions that have been susceptible to conflict over the past year, such rates have hit untypically high levels as there is more nuanced discussion and difficulties with concern to issues of press freedom and ethical journalism. By utilizing this data visualization, one can see where these issues have been under more strain in 2024 and the current state of journalists worldwide.
